About me

I am a practicing psychologist, energy practitioner, and coach who supports people on their journey of inner healing and growth.

My path began with deep personal transformation and continued in India, where I spent 7 years practicing and studying at EKAM — one of the well-known spiritual centers.

Today, I help adults cope with anxiety, loss, inner emptiness, family difficulties, and a loss of meaning.

I create a safe space where you can hear the voice of your soul, restore your inner boundaries, and reconnect with your true self.

In my work, I use transactional and existential analysis, as well as knowledge of family psychology, cognitive-behavioral therapy methods, metaphorical associative cards (MAC), and more.

My professional foundation includes three key areas: mathematics, law, and psychology. This unique combination gives me a special depth and structure in working with clients.

Mathematics
Taught me to think systemically, see hidden connections, and build both external and internal logic. In my client work, I often notice internal "formulas" of behavior a person lives by and gently help bring them to awareness and transformation.

Law
Gave me a clear understanding of boundaries, structure, and responsibility. With over 15 years of legal practice, I’ve learned to support people calmly and precisely in crisis situations. This experience helps me understand clients’ inner experiences more deeply — especially in acute or legal contexts.

Psychology
Is my conscious choice in mature adulthood. Thanks to life, professional, and spiritual experience, I came to psychological practice as a calling that integrates everything I’ve studied — logic, law, and soul.

My strength as a psychologist lies in the combination of deep empathy and clear structure. I know how to be gentle yet precise, supportive yet guiding.
I hear not only words — I sense the inner logic of pain, fear, and confusion — and help navigate through it.
