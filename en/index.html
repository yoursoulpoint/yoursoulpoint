Saule Esenzholova

I am a practicing psychologist, energy practitioner, and coach, supporting people on their path to inner healing and growth.

My journey began with deep personal transformation and continued in India, where I spent seven years studying and practicing at EKAM — one of the well-known spiritual centers.

Today, I help adults cope with anxiety, loss, inner emptiness, family difficulties, and a loss of meaning.

I create a safe space where you can hear the voice of your soul, restore your inner boundaries, and return to your authentic self.

In my practice, I use transactional, and existential approaches, along with knowledge of family psychology, cognitive behavioral therapy, metaphorical cards, and more.
